Endoscopy

Crucial component of Life Care's Medical Imagine services, our ultrasound and endoscopy unit provides diagnostic imagine including Ultrasound Scanning, Doppler Scanning, Echocardiography, Endoscopy, and Colonoscopy services. The unit combines the use of ultra-modern equipment with the expertise of highly-experienced specialist consultants, and is offered at almost all our branches currently.

Radiology

Our Radiology Unit is an integral part of our Medical Imagine services. The Life Care Hospital Kalutara, Piliyandala and Kandy X-ray units are equipped with state-of-the-art Computed Radiographic Systems. Our highly versatile digitizers allow for the production of ultra-clear radiographic images. A distinct advantage of the ultra-modern systems we utilize is that they ensure the film is available within a short period, while maintaining great accuracy and image clarity.
